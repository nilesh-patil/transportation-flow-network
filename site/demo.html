Every claim in the findings is a statement about geography, so here is the geography itself. The map holds all 263 TLC taxi zones. Pick a metric and the zones recolor; hover any zone for its name, borough, and the value being shown. The data is the same yellow-taxi network the rest of the site is built from, with its same honest limit: yellow cabs over-sample Manhattan and the airports, so the outer boroughs are thin by construction, not by accident.

Originally a 2016-17 student project on NYC taxi transportation networks; rebuilt as a reproducible pipeline in the 2021 modernization. Source on GitHub. Set with Newsreader; figures regenerated from 142.2M trips.
